Who I am

Three facets, one path

teacher

Long-standing experience in primary school and ICT, focused on young children and real classroom contexts. I've taught ICT at Colégio Infante D. Henrique, in Funchal, since 2009.

pedagogue

I value active learning pedagogies, project work, cooperation, autonomy, and formative assessment. I aim to create situations where students can investigate, build, experiment, explain, share, and improve their work with their peers.

tech maker

I explore programming, Linux, Python, responsible AI, Minecraft Education, robotics, and digital tools for concrete school problems — small solutions that help students and teachers organise the learning process more clearly.

I use technology as a means to give children more authorship, voice, and autonomy — not as an end in itself.

About me

Technology with purpose

I'm an Information and Communication Technologies teacher at Colégio Infante D. Henrique, in Funchal. I've been at the school since 2006 and, since 2009, I've focused my practice on ICT with young children.

I'm not just interested in teaching tools; I'm interested in creating situations where students can investigate, build, experiment, explain, share, and improve their work. Over the years I've explored digital platforms, programming, educational robotics, Minecraft Education, 3D printing, computational thinking, and responsible artificial intelligence applied to education.
